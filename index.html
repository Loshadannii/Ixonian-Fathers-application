Welcome to the Founding Author Application

We are very pleased that you have chosen to apply to work with Ixonian. Becoming a Founding Author gives early writers a special opportunity to grow their career, build their reputation, and become part of Ixonian from the beginning.

Verified Founding Author Status

Accepted Founding Authors will automatically qualify for a verified and trusted author account. This means your profile, books, and content may receive stronger visibility and recommendations across Ixonian for a set period of time.

Founding Authors will not need to spend credits to receive this verified status during the founding period. However, all accounts and uploaded content must still follow Ixonian's rules, quality standards, and content requirements.

Special Author Titles and Recognition

Ixonian will offer special author titles based on audience growth, achievements, sales, quality, and contribution to the platform. These titles may include:

  • Founding Author
  • Author of the Year
  • Author of the Month
  • Voice of Fiction
  • Voice of Romance
  • Voice of True Crime
  • Legacy Author

These titles are designed to help authors stand out and build a stronger public profile on Ixonian.

Founding Author Credit Rewards

The top 15 Founding Authors who show the most dedication, quality, and willingness to grow with Ixonian may receive a set amount of Ixonian credits.

These credits can be used to help promote their books, grow their profile, unlock special features, or invest further into their author career on the platform. The credit reward is meant to support participation while still keeping the platform fair for all authors.

Increased Book Recommendations

Books uploaded by Founding Authors may be recommended more often for a set amount of time, usually between 1 and 5 years, depending on the author's rank, book quality, audience response, and platform activity.

Each book may be reviewed by the Ixonian team and the Ixonian audience. Based on quality, originality, engagement, and performance, the author's profile may receive a higher ranking on the platform.

We thank all applicants for their cooperation and willingness to help build Ixonian from the beginning.

Limited Founding Author Spots

Applying to become a Founding Author does not guarantee acceptance.

All applications will be reviewed by the Ixonian team. Additional questions may be sent by email if more information is needed. At this stage, Ixonian plans to accept around 70 to 130 Founding Authors.

Earnings From Views and Reads

Audience views, reads, and engagement may be converted into an author balance. Once the balance reaches the required cash-out amount, usually between $30 and $100 USD, authors may be able to withdraw their earnings.

Founding Authors may receive a stronger earning opportunity during their first set period on the platform, usually between 1 and 5 years. Authors may also choose to convert their balance into Ixonian credits to grow their profile, promote books, purchase profile features, unlock tags, or use other author tools.

Referral Rewards

Ixonian authors may receive a personal referral code. When someone uses an author's referral code, the new user may receive a discount, and the Founding Author may earn a small reward, usually between $1 and $2 per successful referral.

There is no fixed limit to how much an author can earn through referrals. Depending on audience size, book performance, and author rank, some authors may earn much more over time.

Original Content Requirement

The use of stolen, copied, or copyrighted content without permission is not allowed. Any author who uploads plagiarized work, copyrighted material, or misleading content may lose their account, credits, earnings, uploaded content, and Founding Author title.

AI-assisted writing must be used responsibly. Authors may not submit fully copied, low-quality, or falsely claimed work as original content.

Author Rights and Publishing License

Authors remain connected to and credited for their books, novels, and original content. By uploading content to Ixonian, authors give Ixonian permission to display, promote, publish, and distribute that content on the platform according to Ixonian's terms.

Authors should only upload work they created themselves or have the legal right to publish.
